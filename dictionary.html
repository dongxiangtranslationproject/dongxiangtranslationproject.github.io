Dongxiang language is a minority language mainly used in Linxia, Gansu and other areas. In history, the writing of Dongxiang language used the Arabic alphabet system to meet the needs of religion and daily records. However, since the 1980s, with the support and promotion of the local government, Dongxiang language has gradually switched to a spelling system based on the internationally recognized 26 Latin letters. This reform has made Dongxiang language more standardized and convenient in writing and dissemination.

At present, a bilingual dictionary of Chinese Dongxiang language has been published. And there are related books that include some sorted bilingual example sentences, providing practical reference tools for learning and communication. At the same time, the compilation of Dongxiang language textbooks has also entered the trial stage, laying the foundation for a more systematic and standardized Dongxiang language education in the future.

东乡语是一种主要使用于甘肃临夏等地的少数民族语言。历史上，东乡语的书写曾采用过阿拉伯字母体系，以满足宗教和日常记录的需要。然而，自二十世纪八十年代以来，在当地政府的支持与推动下，东乡语逐步改用以国际通用的26个拉丁字母为基础的拼写系统。这一改革使东乡语在书写和传播上更加标准化与便捷化。

目前，已有中文—东乡语双语字典问世。并且，也有相关书籍，收录了一部分经过整理的双语例句，为学习与交流提供了实用的参考工具。同时，东乡语教材的编写工作也已进入试行阶段，为未来更系统、更规范的东乡语教育奠定了基础。
