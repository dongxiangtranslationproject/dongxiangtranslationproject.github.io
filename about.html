This platform was initiated to address the long-standing lack of systematic translation tools between the Dongxiang language and Chinese. By leveraging modern technologies in natural language processing and machine learning, we aim to support the preservation, learning, and communication of the Dongxiang language, while contributing to the digital protection and dissemination of minority languages.
