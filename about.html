This platform was co-initiated and developed by Kaixuan Chen (Han) and Bo Ma (Dongxiang) to address the long-standing lack of systematic translation tools between Dongxiang and Chinese/English. We aim to support the preservation, learning, and communication of Dongxiang through technology and promote the digital protection and sustainable dissemination of minority languages.
