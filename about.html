This platform was co-initiated and developed by Kaixuan Chen (Han) and Bo Ma (Dongxiang), with the goal of addressing the long-standing lack of systematic translation tools between the Dongxiang and Chinese/English languages. Through technological means, we aim to support the preservation, learning, and communication of Dongxiang, and to promote the digital protection and sustainable dissemination of minority languages.

Team Members

Kaixuan Chen is an undergraduate at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill (UNC), majoring in Statistics and Biology, Class of 2025. He is currently pursuing a Master's degree in Machine Learning and Data Science (MLDS) at Northwestern University (Class of 2026). His long-standing interests include natural language processing, machine learning, and cross-cultural communication.

Bo Ma is an undergraduate at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill (UNC), majoring in Statistics and, Class of 2025. He is a native speaker of Dongxiang. He has been actively involved in fieldwork and documentation of regional languages and is committed to the digital development of his native language.
